Nitya Nigam

TEACHING

I TA'ed courses in both physics and maths during my undergraduate degree. The resources I devised as a TA can be found here, in the hopes that more people may find them useful!

MATH2000: Introduction to Higher Math

TA for introductory course in proof-based mathematics, designed to introduce students to the language and precsie thinking  of higher mathematics.

Worksheets & solutions

MATH2010: Linear Algebra

TA for generalist introductory course in linear algebra, covering matrices, vector spaces, linear transformations, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, and applications. 

Worksheets & solutions

PHYS1601: Mechanics & Relativity

TA for first-year physics major mechanics, covering fundamental laws, kinematics and dynamics, work and energy, rotational dynamics, oscillations, gravitation, and fluids.

Worksheets & solutions

PHYS1602: Electromagnetism

TA for first-year physics major electromagnetism, covering introductory special relativity, electric fields, direct currents, magnetic fields, alternating currents, and electromagnetic waves.

Worksheets & solutions
