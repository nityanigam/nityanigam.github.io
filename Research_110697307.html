Polarization Design Considerations for the Next Generation Event Horizon Telescope (ngEHT)

Mentored by Dr. Lindy Blackbur​n, Harvard & Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics​

  • ​​Used Fisher analysis to constrain uncertainties on pairs of parameters in a single-baseline model of ngEHT imaging pipeline 
  • Developed an HMC forward model in Python to find covariances between parameter pairs in the 3-baseline case, informing design decisions for the ngEHT including use of mixed-basis measurements and rotation between measurements
  • Presented work at Harvard Black Hole Initiative Group Meeting in July 2022 and AAS 241st Meeting in January 2023

Emission Modeling in the EHT-ngEHT Age

Mentored by Dr. Richard Anantua, Harvard & Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics​ and University of Texas San Antonio

  • Helped address concerns raised in referee report upon submission to journal
