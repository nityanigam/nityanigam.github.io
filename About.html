Nitya Nigam

ABOUT

Hi, I'm Nitya! I'm a junior at Columbia University double majoring in physics and math. I'm broadly interested in theoretical astrophysics and high energy physics, and I'm currently working with Prof. Andrei Beloborodov in Columbia's THEA group. Previously, I worked on polarized imaging of black holes with Dr. Lindy Blackburn and the Next Generation Event Horizon Telescope (ngEHT) collaboration at Harvard's Black Hole Initiative. In Summer 2024 I will be working on tidal disruption events with Prof. Vikram Ravi through Caltech's SURF program.

Aside from science, I'm also an avid musician across a variety of styles. I'm a pianist, vocalist, composer, and conductor, and have been a Co-Artistic Director of Columbia Bach Society since August 2023. In our Spring 2024 concert, I premiered a piece for a cappella choir (to the right). Check out the recording of our Fall 2023 concert here!
