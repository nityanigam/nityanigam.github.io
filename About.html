Nitya Nigam

ABOUT

Hi, I'm Nitya! I'm a MASt Astrophysics student at Trinity College, University of Cambridge. Prior to this, I double majored in physics and maths at Columbia University. I'm broadly interested in theoretical astrophysics, and I'm currently working with Prof. Henrik Latter in the Cambridge DAMTP Astrophysics group on magnetic buoyancy instabilities and turbulence in the intracluster medium. I have previously worked on tidal disruption events with Prof. Vikram Ravi through Caltech's SURF program, MHD for neutron star interiors with Prof. Andrei Beloborodov at Columbia, and on polarized imaging of black holes with Dr. Lindy Blackburn and the Next Generation Event Horizon Telescope (ngEHT) collaboration at Harvard's Black Hole Initiative.

Aside from science, I'm also an avid musician across a variety of styles. I'm a pianist, vocalist, composer, and conductor, and was Co-Artistic Director of Columbia Bach Society from August 2023 to May 2025.
