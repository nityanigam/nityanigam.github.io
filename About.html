Nitya Nigam

ABOUT

Hi, I'm Nitya! I'm a junior at Columbia University double majoring in physics and math. I'm broadly interested in theoretical astrophysics and high energy physics, and I'm currently working with Prof. Andrei Beloborodov in Columbia's THEA group. Previously, I worked on polarized imaging of black holes with Dr. Lindy Blackburn and the Next Generation Event Horizon Telescope (ngEHT) collaboration at Harvard's Black Hole Initiative.

Aside from science, I'm also an avid musician across a variety of styles. I'm a pianist, guitarist, vocalist, and composer, and I've recently gained the privilege of being a Co-Artistic Director with Columbia Bach Society. Check out the recording of our Fall 2023 concert, and some of my original compositions - I'm looking forward to premiering a choral piece I wrote with Bach Society in our Spring 2024 concert!
